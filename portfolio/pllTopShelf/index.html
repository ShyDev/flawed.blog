    PLL Top Shelf

    Platforms: iOS

    Release date: Jul 26, 2021

    Type: commercial release

    PLL TOP SHELF

    Test your shooting skills with the best professional lacrosse players in the world. Play with top Premier Lacrosse league pros like Paul Rabil, Matt Rambo, Tom Schreiber, and more. Collect coins to unlock new players, sticks, and merch.

    SHARP SHOOTER

    Break targets by shooting at them as they show up on the screen. The more targets you break, the more coins you can collect. Hit a bullseye and get more coins!

    UNLOCK YOUR FAVORITE PLAYERS

    Use your coins to get access to your favorite players. New players and jerseys drop weekly so make sure to rack up those coins!

    POWER UPS

    Use powerups to reach your high score quickly or bail yourself out when you get behind. Use multiple powerups in a game to really rack up the coins.

    LEADERBOARDS

    Compete against your friends, family, or other fans in the PLL Universe. Participate in weekly challenges to cement your name as one of the best shooters around!
