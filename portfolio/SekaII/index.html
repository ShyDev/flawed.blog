    Seka II

    Platforms: Steam Demo

    Release date: TBA

    Type: commercial release

    Seka II is a Co-op for 1-8 players

    Embark on a thrilling cooperative adventures in SEKA II, a unique game that challenges you and your friends to become cunning mice on an epic quest for cheese!

    Navigate through intricate labyrinths filled with twists, turns, and devious traps, all set by the mysterious EYE determined to test your wits and agility.

    As you delve deeper into these cheese-infused mazes, you’ll need to rely on teamwork and strategy to outsmart the EYE’s experiments and evade its relentless pursuit. Collaborate with your friends, forming a formidable pack of mice, to gather as much cheese as possible and uncover the secrets hidden within the labyrinth’s walls.

    Exciting co-op gameplay: Join forces with friends to explore, solve puzzles, and escape the gaze of the EYE Dynamic labyrinths: Each maze is filled with surprises and challenges Strategic teamwork: Coordinate with your pack to navigate obstacles, share loot, and outmaneuver the EYE’s traps

    Gather your friends, embrace your inner mouse, and dive into the delightful chaos of SEKA II. Will your pack emerge victorious, or will the EYE’s experiments keep you trapped forever in its cheesy domain? The adventure awaits!
